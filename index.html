The Evolution of Amazon

  • 1994- Founded by Jeff Bezos as an online bookstore
  • 1997- Amazon became a publically traded company
  • 1998- Began selling music and videos
  • 1999- Expanded further, selling things like video games, software and toys
  • 2002- The launch of Amazon Web Services
  • 2006- The first year of Amazon fulfillment
  • 2012- Aquisition of Kiva systems to automate inventory managment
  • 2021- Annoucnement of Jeff Bezos' resignation as CEO
